About Me

Currently...

I am a second-year student in the University of Virginia School for Engineering and Applied Sciences. I am pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science (CS), and concurrently exploring economics, politics, and any other topics I find interesting!

A nerd at heart...

I began taking CS classes in high school. In addition to offerring varied and interesting CS courses, my school encouraged me to take part in hackathons, and gave me the opportunity to conduct my own CS research project during my senior year.

When I'm not coding...

I love to read! At the risk of running headfirst into full-out geek, I am often described by my friends as the girl who would take a book to a party if she could get away with it (they don't need to know I always have a book on my phone, so they're actually right).

I also love volunteering and doing different kinds of community service. I was a Girl Scout for 10 years, and earned my Girl Scout Gold Award after beginning a program to tutor children living in low-income areas.

But are you ever not a nerd?

Dancing is a huge part of my life - I am co-captain of the non-competitive Bollywood dance team at my university - and have been dancing since I learned how to walk. I also enjoy writing, ice-skating, jewelry-making, and watching movies.
