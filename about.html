About Me

Currently...

I am a second-year student in the University of Virginia School for Engineering and Applied Sciences. I am pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science (CS), and concurrently exploring economics, politics, and any other topics I find interesting!

Computer Science experience...

I began taking CS classes in high school. In addition to offerring varied CS courses, my school gave me the opportunity to conduct a research project during my senior year. I have extensive experience with Java, Python, and C, and in both Android and responsive web app development.

When I'm not coding...

I love to read and write! I also enjoy volunteering and doing different kinds of community service. I was a Girl Scout for 10 years, and earned my Girl Scout Gold Award after beginning a program to tutor children living in low-income areas.

Beyond academics...
